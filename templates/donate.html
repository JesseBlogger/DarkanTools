What is this fund?
If this experiment is successful it may be possible to restore classic RS 2012 websites like tip.it, Sal's Realm, runehq and possibly a wiki if they are available on wayback machine.

Does out community need to do work?
I(jawarrior1) would do the heavy lifting and host the websites themselves, I just ask for a little help on the funding. If this exeriment is unsuccessful I will post the results on the Discord. If it is successful I will restore Sal's Realm and RuneHQ with similar funding at $15 each.

Who can benefit?
-Everyone on Darkan can enjoy RS 2012 more.
-Trent may benefit in understanding RS 2012, which can help development

What are the risks?
-Even restored the websites may not tell us anything new, so they may be completely useless.
-The restoration may come out a mess and be unusable.

How will this be done?
An automated service automatically restores websites from WayBackMachine for $15 each. We take the website files and upload them to a cloud for our reference.
